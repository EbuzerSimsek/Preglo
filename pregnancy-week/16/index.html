What happens in week 16

You may notice slight flutters soon; baby can hear muffled sounds. You are in the second trimester. Every pregnancy is different — use these notes as a gentle orientation, then log how your week feels in Preglo.

Baby size at week 16

Average estimates put length around 11.6 cm and weight around 100 g (roughly apple). Ultrasound dating and genetics can shift these numbers.

What to track this week

  • Symptoms, mood, and energy in your daily log
  • Water intake toward a simple daily goal
  • Appointment dates and questions for your care provider
  • Photos or notes you want to keep private on your phone
