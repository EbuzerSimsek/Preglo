What happens in week 24

Viability thresholds improve with medical care; still early. You are in the second trimester. Every pregnancy is different — use these notes as a gentle orientation, then log how your week feels in Preglo.

Baby size at week 24

Average estimates put length around 30.0 cm and weight around 600 g (roughly ear of corn). Ultrasound dating and genetics can shift these numbers.

What to track this week

  • Symptoms, mood, and energy in your daily log
  • Water intake toward a simple daily goal
  • Kick sessions if movements are regular (often later pregnancy)
  • Photos or notes you want to keep private on your phone

Tools that help

Use the due date calculator if you are still estimating timing, browse the full week-by-week guide, or open the kick counter when movements become a daily habit.
