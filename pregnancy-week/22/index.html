Baby development at week 22

Grasping reflexes appear. Eyes may respond to light changes even while closed. Hearing continues to sharpen to your voice and household sounds.

Average size estimates for week 22 are about 27.8 cm and 430 g — roughly carrot. Your ultrasound report and clinician’s dating override any website average.

What you might feel

Skin stretching, itchy belly, or hip discomfort can increase. Moisturizer and side-sleeping habits help many people.

You are in the second trimester. Two pregnancies at the same week can feel completely different — treat these notes as orientation, not a checklist you must match.

Noticing movement in week 22

Flutters often become clearer around mid-pregnancy, though timing varies widely. You do not need a formal count yet for every pregnancy — casual awareness is enough. When patterns feel regular later, Preglo’s kick counter is ready without a separate app.
