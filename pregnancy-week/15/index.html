Baby development at week 15

Leg bones lengthen. Blood vessels show through thin skin. Hearing structures continue maturing so muffled sounds may register later.

Average size estimates for week 15 are about 10.1 cm and 70 g — roughly apple. Your ultrasound report and clinician’s dating override any website average.

What you might feel

You might notice a glow — or acne, or both. Skin changes are hormonal and temporary for many.

You are in the second trimester. Two pregnancies at the same week can feel completely different — treat these notes as orientation, not a checklist you must match.

What to track this week

  • Symptoms, mood, sleep, and energy in your daily log
  • Water intake toward a simple daily goal
  • Appointment dates and questions for your care provider

Week 15 FAQ

How big is the baby at week 15?

Typical educational averages are about 10.1 cm long and 70 g. Individual growth and measurement methods vary.

Which trimester is week 15?

Week 15 is generally counted in the second trimester (weeks 1–12 first, 13–27 second, 28–40 third in common educational grouping).
