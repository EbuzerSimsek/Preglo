Baby development at week 23

Rapid eye movement patterns emerge. Lungs produce early surfactant ingredients while practicing inhale/exhale motions with fluid — not air yet.

Average size estimates for week 23 are about 28.9 cm and 501 g — roughly carrot. Your ultrasound report and clinician’s dating override any website average.

What you might feel

Swollen ankles by evening are common. Elevate feet when you can and ask your clinician what swelling level should prompt a call.

You are in the second trimester. Two pregnancies at the same week can feel completely different — treat these notes as orientation, not a checklist you must match.

Noticing movement in week 23

Flutters often become clearer around mid-pregnancy, though timing varies widely. You do not need a formal count yet for every pregnancy — casual awareness is enough. When patterns feel regular later, Preglo’s kick counter is ready without a separate app.
