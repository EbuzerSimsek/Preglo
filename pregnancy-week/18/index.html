Baby development at week 18

Myelin begins coating nerves. Ears stand out more. Many anatomy ultrasounds happen around weeks 18–22 depending on local practice.

Average size estimates for week 18 are about 14.2 cm and 190 g — roughly pear. Your ultrasound report and clinician’s dating override any website average.

What you might feel

Backaches and hip looseness can start. Sleep positions may need pillows and experimentation.

You are in the second trimester. Two pregnancies at the same week can feel completely different — treat these notes as orientation, not a checklist you must match.

What to track this week

  • Symptoms, mood, sleep, and energy in your daily log
  • Water intake toward a simple daily goal
  • Appointment dates and questions for your care provider
  • Scan dates and follow-up notes after ultrasound visits

Typical educational averages are about 14.2 cm long and 190 g. Individual growth and measurement methods vary.

Which trimester is week 18?

Week 18 is generally counted in the second trimester (weeks 1–12 first, 13–27 second, 28–40 third in common educational grouping).
