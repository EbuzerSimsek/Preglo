Kick counter

Baby kick counter for clearer movement tracking

Count rolls, jabs, and flutters in a focused session — then keep that history next to your week tracker and daily notes.

Why parents use a kick counter

A baby kick counter helps you notice your baby’s usual movement pattern over time. Preglo makes sessions simple: start, tap for each movement, and save the result.

Because kick tracking lives inside your pregnancy app, you do not need a separate tool for later pregnancy.

How Preglo’s kick counter works

  • Start a timed kick session
  • Tap for each movement you feel
  • Review past sessions in your history
  • Keep the rest of your pregnancy log in the same place

Tips for everyday use

Pick a quiet moment, often later in the day, and use a consistent position when you can. If something feels different from your baby’s normal pattern, contact your care provider — Preglo is for personal tracking only.

FAQ

What is a baby kick counter?

A kick counter is a simple tool for logging fetal movements during a timed session so you can notice patterns over days and weeks.

Does Preglo include kick history?

Yes. Preglo saves kick sessions so you can look back while also tracking your week, symptoms, and other pregnancy notes.
