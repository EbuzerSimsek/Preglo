How this due date calculator works

Most calculators add about 280 days (40 weeks) from the first day of your last menstrual period, then adjust if your cycle is longer or shorter than 28 days. Conception often happens around ovulation — roughly mid-cycle — so LMP dating is an estimate, not a lab result.

Ultrasound dating

Early ultrasound measurements can refine your due date. If your clinician gives a different date, use that in Preglo. This tool never replaces medical dating.

FAQ

Is a due date exact?

No. Only a small share of babies arrive on the exact due date. It is a planning midpoint.

What if my cycles are irregular?

LMP math is less reliable. Ask your clinician about ultrasound dating and enter their date in Preglo.
