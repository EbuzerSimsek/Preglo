Kick awareness

Reduced fetal movement

Practical guidance for personal pregnancy tracking — then log sessions in Preglo when you are ready.

Why movement patterns matter

Babies have quieter and more active periods. What matters most is a clear change from your baby’s usual pattern — fewer or weaker movements than you expect.

What to do if movements feel reduced

  1. Stop and focus in a quiet place; try a side-lying position.
  2. Start a kick session and note what you feel.
  3. If movements still feel reduced or you are worried, contact your midwife, doctor, or maternity triage — do not wait for an app.

How Preglo helps (and what it does not)

Preglo stores kick sessions so you can remember recent patterns. It does not diagnose complications and never replaces urgent clinical care. Pair awareness tools with professional guidance.

FAQ

Should I wait until tomorrow?

If you are concerned about reduced movements, contact your care provider the same day — do not delay for an app score.

Can stress change movements?

Activity, position, and meals can change what you feel, but worry itself is a reason to check with a clinician.
