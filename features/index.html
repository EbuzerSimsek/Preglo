Features

Preglo features for a complete pregnancy companion

All the everyday tools in one private iPhone app — built to feel calm, clear, and useful.

Core tracking

  • Pregnancy week & due date progress
  • Baby kick counter
  • Contraction timer
  • Daily mood, symptoms, and notes
  • Water intake goals
  • Bump photo timeline

Health & devices

  • Weight, blood pressure, and blood sugar logs

  • Home screen widgets for week and days to go

Your data stays on your device by default. Preglo is for personal tracking and general wellness only — not medical advice.
