Kick counter apps

Best baby kick counter apps

A practical look at pregnancy tracking tools — written for parents who want clarity without hype.

Must-have features in a kick counter

  • One-tap movement logging during a timed or count-based session
  • History you can revisit when a clinician asks “how has movement been?”
  • Works offline on your phone
  • Lives next to your week tracker so context is not scattered
  • Clear boundaries — awareness tool, not a diagnostic device

Preglo’s approach

Preglo includes a dedicated kick counter inside a full pregnancy companion: week progress, water, symptoms, contractions, and widgets. That means kick sessions stay part of the same private timeline.

Before you rely on any method, read how to count baby kicks and the 10-kick count method. If movements feel reduced, prioritize clinical advice — see reduced fetal movement.

Standalone counters vs all-in-one apps

A tiny standalone counter can work for a week. Most parents eventually want history plus pregnancy context. All-in-one apps reduce app-switching and keep your notes in one place — which is the problem Preglo is designed to solve.
